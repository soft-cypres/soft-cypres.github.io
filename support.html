FAQ

Common questions.

What is an AAD?

An Automatic Activation Device is a safety unit worn on a parachute rig that automatically deploys the reserve canopy if a skydiver is still falling above a certain speed below a certain altitude. The CYPRES 2 is one of the most widely used AADs in the sport.

Is this the real CYPRES?

No. Soft-CYPRES is a simulator — a training tool that mirrors the look, controls, and behaviour of the real CYPRES 2 Changeable Mode unit so you can practise safely. It is not produced by, endorsed by, or affiliated with Airtec GmbH. It is not a substitute for official CYPRES training or for using the real equipment.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The entire app works without an internet connection. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is downloaded after install. Fly with it, train with it, use it anywhere.

Does the app collect any data?

No. No accounts, no analytics, no trackers, no third-party SDKs. Your preferences (language, haptics, theme) are stored only on your device. See our privacy policy for the full statement.

Why does haptic feedback feel different on Android?

On iPhone, the Taptic Engine can produce genuinely distinct Light, Medium, and Heavy impacts — so we offer all four settings. On Android, most vibration motors can't differentiate those levels, so we surface a simple On/Off toggle instead. "On" maps to the medium setting internally.

How do I change language?

Open Settings from the home screen, then pick your language from the list. Choices include English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, and Russian. Terminology in each language follows the official CYPRES 2 manual for that region.

How do I reset the simulator to defaults?

Settings → Reset Simulator. This clears your saved profile and restores the unit to factory-fresh state. Useful if you want to practise the full first-time setup flow.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email vistas.promo-7e@icloud.com with your device model, OS version, and the steps that led to the issue. A screenshot or short screen recording helps enormously.

Will there be more lessons and scenarios?

Yes — new scenarios are added in ongoing updates. If you have a location, hazard, or drop-zone situation you'd like to see turned into a training scenario, send us the idea. We've already built scenarios in Switzerland, Singapore, Monaco, the Seychelles, Eloy, and many more.
