What it is

Button-for-button fidelity, without the unit on your bench.

Becoming fluent with the CYPRES 2 control sequence is quickest when you can rehearse freely. Soft-CYPRES recreates the display, the press timings, the LED feedback, and every operating mode — so you can practise at home, on a plane, or between jumps without ever touching a live unit.

Lessons

Learn by doing. Eight guided lessons across three skill tiers.

Each lesson walks you through a single skill with immediate feedback — press, hold, release, confirm. No manuals to memorise, no videos to scrub through.

Playground

The full device, with no rails.

Explore every state of the unit at your own pace. No lesson, no scenario — just you and the CYPRES 2.

  • Toggle power and watch the self-test sequence
  • Cycle unit info: flights, serial, service date, unit, altitude, mode
  • Change operating mode — Expert, Student, Tandem, Speed
  • Dial activation altitude from A0 to A9
  • Set a dropzone offset in feet or meters
  • Watch the 14-hour auto-power-off countdown run in real time

Control panel

Press timings, LED signalling, and self-test — recreated.

The simulator distinguishes stab (short press) from press-hold and reacts the way the real unit does. Every LED flash, every display transition, every mode indicator is modelled after the actual CYPRES 2.

Stab vs press-hold

Quick tap for selection, sustained hold for navigation — the same threshold your fingers need to learn on a real unit.

Self-test on power up

Four sequential LED flashes, just like the real start-up sequence. Confirms readiness before any mode changes.

14-hour auto power-off

Live countdown shown on screen. Matches the real unit's behaviour — or toggle it off in settings for practice.

A0 through A9

Ten activation-altitude steps, 30 ft each, adjustable with the authentic press-hold-select flow.

Four operating modes

Expert, Student, Tandem, Speed — each with its own deployment profile and mode-change confirmation.

Dropzone offset

Set the elevation difference between exit and landing in ±30 ft (or ±10 m) increments.

Design

Dark, tactile, deliberate.

The interface matches the spirit of the hardware: a metal-finish display under glass, magenta-accented controls, spring-physics motion, and a quiet dark aesthetic that stays out of your way.

Glassmorphism layers

Translucent pills and cards layered over a deep gradient — so information feels lifted off the surface.

Spring animations

Every transition uses physics-based motion with staggered entry. Nothing snaps, nothing lags.

Haptic feedback

Four intensity levels on iPhone's Taptic Engine. A simple on/off toggle on Android, since typical vibration motors can't differentiate strengths.

Dark mode by default

Matches the real-unit aesthetic and is kind to your eyes on a plane ride. Light mode is one tap away in settings.

Languages

Six locales. Manual-accurate terminology.

Every language uses the exact terminology from the official CYPRES 2 manual for that region — so what you learn here is what you'll see and hear at the DZ.

Offline & private

No accounts. No tracking. Nothing to leak.

Soft-CYPRES runs entirely on your device. There is no sign-up, no network requests, no analytics, no third-party SDKs that collect data. Your language choice, your haptic preference, your last-used settings — stored on your phone, nowhere else.
